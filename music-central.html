📖 How to use Music Central?
⌄

Type the song name and the artist name in the fields, then click Analyze Music. The tool will search databases and return the album cover, metadata and lyrics automatically.

Use the Lyrics, Metadata and Similar tabs to explore different information about the track. You can copy the lyrics to your clipboard or download them as a .txt file.

Tip: Press Enter after typing to search without clicking the button. Your recent searches are saved automatically and appear below the results.
